The Salvation Army's Massachusetts Division "Hunger Help Hope" direct mail package combines several proven fundraising techniques into a single appeal. The response device leads with a matching gift incentive — "your gift will be matched 3-to-1 for the next three months, up to a total of $300,000" — transforming each dollar into a multiplied impact. The package includes a donation form with suggested amounts, a self-mailer with program information, and emotional photography of children and families served.

The "Triple Impact Campaign" framing is a textbook application of the matching gift principle: donors give more when they believe their contribution will be amplified. The Salvation Army, one of the largest and most recognizable charities in the world, has refined its direct mail program over decades. This Massachusetts Division package shows how a national brand adapts its messaging for regional campaigns while maintaining the core elements that drive response — urgency, tangibility, and the promise that every dollar goes further.
