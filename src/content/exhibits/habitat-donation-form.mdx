Habitat for Humanity's modern donation form showcases the full evolution of online giving technology. The form guides donors through a complete payment flow — from selecting a giving amount to entering payment details — in a single, uninterrupted experience. This end-to-end approach represents a significant departure from the early days of online fundraising, when donors were often shuttled between multiple pages, payment processors, and confirmation screens, losing momentum and abandoning the process at each transition.

The form's design reflects Habitat's unique position in the nonprofit landscape. As an organization built on the tangible act of building homes, Habitat has always excelled at connecting donations to concrete outcomes. The modern form extends this concreteness into the digital experience: clear amounts, transparent processing, and immediate confirmation. The payment flow integrates modern options that would have been unimaginable in the early days of online giving, when the Red Cross first experimented with SSL-secured donation pages. Habitat's form is the product of two decades of iteration across the nonprofit sector — every design choice informed by conversion data, donor research, and the accumulated knowledge of what moves people from consideration to action.
