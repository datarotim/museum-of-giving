Zeffy — 100% Free Fundraising Platform

Zeffy · 2022 · US
Web

Zeffy made fundraising software free by asking donors to tip the platform instead. Zero fees to nonprofits meant even the smallest organizations could accept online donations without losing a percentage.

Zeffy launched with a proposition that challenged the established fundraising technology business model: a fully featured donation platform with zero fees for nonprofits. No subscription, no transaction fees, no percentage of donations taken. The platform sustained itself through voluntary tips from donors, who could add an optional contribution to Zeffy at checkout.

The model directly addressed a persistent pain point for small and mid-size nonprofits: the feeling that platform fees eroded donor generosity. A 5% platform fee on a $50 donation meant $2.50 that didn’t reach the cause — a reality that frustrated both organizations and donors. Zeffy’s approach democratized access to professional fundraising tools, enabling organizations with minimal budgets to offer a modern donation experience. The tip model also introduced an interesting behavioral dynamic: many donors voluntarily added a platform tip, suggesting that transparency about costs and the freedom to choose generated goodwill. Zeffy demonstrated that the business model of fundraising technology was itself ripe for innovation.
