The 2024 charity: water donation form is everything a century of fundraising innovation built toward: beautiful, fast, personal, transparent, mobile-native, and one tap from complete.

charity: water’s 2024 donation form represents the current state of the art in nonprofit fundraising UX. The form is mobile-first, loads instantly, and offers Apple Pay and Google Pay as the primary payment options. Suggested amounts are tied to specific impact metrics (“$40 can give one person clean water”). A recurring gift toggle is prominently featured. The design is minimal, the copy is precise, and every element serves a conversion purpose.

The mobile wallet integration embodies the friction-reduction imperative that has driven every technological advance in giving. Stand in front of this exhibit and you see not just a donation form — you see the entire history of fundraising, compressed into a single interface.
